Digital Solution for a private chef

This project is centered around Vivian Andersen (ACEN), a 25 year old professional gourmet chef based in Copenhagen. The goal is to create a website, the brand identity, the social media content, guideline and strategy that are aligned with the client’s values and long term goals. The website serves the role of a “digital business card” with a minimalistic design, an easy navigation system, where customers can have easy access to a portfolio of our client’s work, find useful information about the service she provides (menus, prices, social media links, must-knows, reviews), and last but not least provide a direct contact link (contact form/ booking system). It is primarily a school exam with specific requirements and a brief allocated time for completion, therefore the solution presented is a diamond in the rough. The team will be coding the website from scratch in limited time according to the exam requirements, but to fit our clients needs and knowledge, e a different version using Wordpress, Shopify or Wix would need to be set in place so that she can maintain and update herself.
